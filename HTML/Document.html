Hackers

Hackers strike fear in almost half of big businesses. 10/2/2018.

A recent survey from data-tracking company Ensighten
found that 46% of all enterprise brands are concerned about
data breaches after other large companies,
such as Facebook and Uber, were hacked.
Still, 67% have no marketing security in place, the survey found.

Blokchain

The world’s two largest enterprise blockchain groups join forces. 10/2/2018.

The Enterprise Ethereum Alliance and Hyperledger will combine forces to create an open-source,
standards-based cross-platform project to bring businesses distributed ledger solutions.
“At Hyperledger, we’re very focused at building communities that build a set of software products and
many of those touch on and implement Ethereumrelated standards, and the EEA is focused on driving a
community of organizations around a common set of standards and then certifying applications against those standards”.,
Says EEA Executive Director Ron Resnick.
