Koprivnica

Koprivnica is a city in Northern Croatia, located 70 kilometers northeast of Zagreb.
It is the capital and the largest city of the Koprivnica-Kri≈æevci county.
In 2011, the city's administrative area of 90.94 km2 had a total population of 30,854, with 23,955 in the city proper.

Koprivnica is situated at a strategic location on the slopes of Bilogora and Kalnik to the south and river Drava to the north.
Its position enabled it to develop numerous amenities for the wider area such as trade, crafts and administration,
and in the 13th century Koprivnica became a town settlement.
Koprivnica was named after the stream of the same name, which was first mentioned at the beginning of the 13th century during the Hungarian Kingdom.
